Key Features for Executive Presence

1. The Sticky Sentences Report (The "Decisiveness" Filter)

Every sentence is composed of "working words" (the core meaning) and "glue words" (the filler: the, is, of, that, which, really, very).

The Problem: Non-native speakers often rely on a high percentage of glue words to "play it safe." This makes your writing feel sluggish.

The Fix: PWA highlights sentences with more than 40% glue words. By reducing this percentage, your prose becomes punchy and authoritative.

2. The Contextual Thesaurus & Rephraser

Generic AI often suggests "smart-sounding" words that are incorrect for a corporate context. PWA’s rephraser allows you to toggle between Professional, Formal, and Casual tones. It suggests high-impact verbs like orchestrate, implement, or leverage instead of simple alternatives.

3. Sentence Length & Variety

In an executive summary, short, declarative sentences signal confidence. ProWritingAid provides a visual "heartbeat" graph of your sentence lengths. PWA prompts you to vary your cadence, ensuring your most important points land with maximum impact.
