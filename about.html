About EnglishFix

A practical guide for non-native speakers who want better English without wasting time on low-quality tools.

My Story

I created EnglishFix after seeing students and professionals struggle with unclear writing, expensive subscriptions, and generic advice. This site focuses on practical recommendations that actually improve real-world communication.

How I Choose Recommendations

  • Real-use testing on email, essays, and business writing.
  • Clarity of corrections and rewrite quality.
  • Value for money on free and paid plans.
  • Beginner-friendliness and learning curve.
