About EnglishFix

EnglishFix exists for one simple reason:

to help non-native English speakers speak and write English confidently—without overthinking every sentence.

If you've ever second-guessed an email, rewritten the same sentence five times, or worried about sounding "wrong," you're exactly who this site is for.

Who I Am

I'm Richard, the person behind EnglishFix.

I've spent decades writing—technical documentation, blogs, and content that actually has to make sense to real people. On top of that, I've worked as an English content editor for a language learning publisher, helping shape materials used by learners around the world.

I've also taught English as a foreign language, so I've seen firsthand where people struggle—and more importantly, what actually works.

No fluff. No academic theory for the sake of it. Just practical English that people can use.

What EnglishFix Does

EnglishFix focuses on one thing:

helping you communicate clearly and confidently in English.

  • Fixing common writing mistakes
  • Improving clarity and natural flow
  • Learning how native speakers actually use English
  • Using tools (like grammar checkers and writing assistants) properly—not blindly

There's a lot of bad advice out there. This site cuts through it.

Who This Is For

EnglishFix is mainly for non-native English speakers, but there are two main groups that benefit the most:

Students

If you're writing essays, assignments, or exams, you'll find practical help to:

  • avoid common mistakes
  • improve structure and clarity
  • get better results without memorising endless rules

Professionals

If you use English at work, this is where things really matter.

You'll learn how to:

  • write clear emails
  • sound natural (not robotic)
  • communicate confidently with colleagues and clients

Why This Site Exists

Let's be honest.

EnglishFix started because I wanted to turn what I already know into something that actually makes money online.

I've spent years working with English. It made no sense to ignore that and start from scratch.

I originally began creating worksheets and teaching materials, but that's a slow game. You can spend months building something before finding out if anyone even wants it.

So I pivoted.

This site is about faster feedback, real-world usefulness, and building something that works—both for you and for me.

help you stop worrying about your English and start using it confidently.
