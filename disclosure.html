The EnglishFix Transparency Commitment

At EnglishFix, our mission is to provide high-level English learners with the best possible tools to find their professional voice. To keep our deep-dive guides and resources free for everyone, we participate in several affiliate marketing programs.

How it works:

When you click on a link to a tool or service recommended on this site and make a purchase, we may receive a small commission from the provider.

Our Promise to You:

  • No Extra Cost

    You never pay more by using our links. In fact, we often negotiate exclusive discounts for the EnglishFix community.

  • Integrity First

    We only recommend tools that we have personally tested and believe will genuinely help you improve your professional communication.

  • Independence

    Our reviews are not influenced by the commissions we receive. If a tool is difficult to use or overpriced, we will tell you.

Thank you for supporting the library and helping us keep these resources available to professionals worldwide.
